history

  • 2002 set up as a database in order to manage the inventarisation of about 6.000 headstones from the Ashkenazic Cemetery in Hamburg-Altona (1621-1871)
  • data ingest by team of 10-12 researchers
  • remote access | server based
  • since 2006 online
  • more projects followed: ongoing development of epidat is driven by projects and cooperations
  • since 2019 developed in cooperation between Steinheim-Institute, Essen and Academy of Science and Literature | Mainz
